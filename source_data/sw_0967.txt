I Swore I Would Never Get This Bad Again

I came to college thinking I could conquer the world. This was it. This was what I wanted. This was the school I wanted and it was the perfect distance away from home. 
But then it got bad again. The excitement faded and I am left feeling dead inside. I have fallen into bad habits with cutting and starving and purging if I do eat. I talk to the counselor on campus but I am finding it difficult to open up. 
And slowly but surely the thought of killing myself has crept into my mind. I don't have a plan or a date set. But one of my close friends is coming down from Georgia to visit me in early november and I would like to see her before I do it. I feel so selfish but at the same time I don't know if I can handle this pain/
